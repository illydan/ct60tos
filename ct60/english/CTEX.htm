CT60

Expander

 

This expander allows to move the CT60 at the rear of the Falcon case to avoid all place problems under the keyboard with the Fan and the CT60 add-on cards...

CTEX allows too to plug a Falcon daughter card forward the CT60 without soldering some mal/mal connectors !
