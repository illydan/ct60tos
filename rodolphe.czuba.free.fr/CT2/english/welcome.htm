CT2

Falcon 030 Accelerator Board

 

CT2 Rev B

The CT2 was designed and sold in two versions :

- Rev A : Designed from January 1997 to June 98 and sold (92 units) from September 98 to December 98

- Rev B : Designed from December 98 to January 99 and sold (150 units) from February 99 to January 00.

Because of the high technical goals, it was at this time the most ambitious hardware project ever launched on Falcon market.

A total of 242 units have been sold from September 1998 to January 2000 in 14 countries.

In 2003, the CT2 was replaced by the powerfull CT60...
