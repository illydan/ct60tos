Connect the IRQ from CTPCI IDE port :

Solder the wire on the right top corner of the CTPCI (pad is here for that and named IRQ).
The other end of wire must be connected on IRQ on Falcon Motherboard.
You have choice : connect on wire #31 of the Falcon IDE flat cable
or solder on pin #1 of the U25 chip (74HCT04) near Zilog (under the top position of the CT60).
