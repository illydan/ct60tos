Note about MAGXBOOT.PRG
-----------------------
MAGXBOOT is updated in this archive 0.99e for RGB monitors (external 
clock used at 32 MHz) and a new FPU patch (FPU context bug with the 
68060 and the preemptive mode). Patches are also updated for MagiC 
5.13 but this version is not tested.
